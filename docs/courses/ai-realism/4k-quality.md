The Technical Differences That Matter
Two upgrades create immediate impact:

1080p → 4K
Higher resolution increases clarity, depth, and micro-detail. Skin texture, lighting transitions, and product edges become sharper and more believable.

30fps → 60fps
Higher frame rate improves motion smoothness. Movements feel natural instead of mechanical or jittery.

These are not cosmetic improvements.
They change perceived realism.

Understanding Upscaling (Beginner Breakdown)

Upscaling is not magic.
It is refinement.

Upscaling means:

Increasing resolution

Improving sharpness

Enhancing micro-details

Smoothing perceived motion

Reducing compression artifacts

It does not change the story.
It does not alter the content.

It upgrades presentation.

What Upscaling Actually Does
When you upscale a video:

Edges become cleaner

Textures become more defined

Faces appear sharper

Motion feels more fluid (especially when paired with higher frame rates)

The structure remains the same.
The perception improves.

Where to Upscale Your AI Video

Follow these steps to properly upscale your video:

1️⃣ Open Video Upscale (Inside Higgsfield)
Navigate to:
Video → Video Upscale

2️⃣ Upload Your Base Video
Upload the video you generated in Module 2.

3️⃣ Select the Topaz Model
Choose Topaz as the upscaling model.

Why Topaz?
Topaz is preferred for AI-generated content because it:

Preserves facial detail accurately

Enhances skin texture without over-smoothing

Improves perceived motion smoothness

Maintains structural integrity of AI-rendered frames

It is widely used in professional workflows for a reason.

If your goal is cinematic polish rather than raw export quality,
this is the correct upgrade step.
