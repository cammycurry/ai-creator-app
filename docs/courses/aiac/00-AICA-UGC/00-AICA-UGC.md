**Update: Better Workflow for UGC**

The 9:16 ratio is now available, and it seems this workflow works best for getting your model hold what you want:

- Instead of Nano Banana, you can use Nano Banana PRO for even more realism.

- Make sure you have the **final image of your model** ready, posed exactly how you want her to be.

- **Upload that image** into the **first field** inside the Omni Reference box.

- **Upload the product image** into the **second box**.

When typing your prompt, say something like:

- *“She is holding that.”*

- *“She is holding that parfume.”*
(Adjust your prompt depending on the image you’re using.)
