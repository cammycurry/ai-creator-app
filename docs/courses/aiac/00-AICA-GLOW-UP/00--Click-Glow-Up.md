**Update: Better Workflow for Dressing Your Model**

The 9:16 ratio is now available, and it seems this workflow works best for getting your model to wear what you want:

- Instead of Nano Banana, you can use Nano Banana PRO for even more realism.

- Make sure you have the **final image of your model** ready, posed exactly how you want her to be.

- **Upload that image** into the **first field** inside the Omni Reference box.

- **Upload the clothing image** into the **second box**, and make sure the image is in **9:16 ratio**.

When typing your prompt, say something like:

- *“She is wearing all of that.”*

- *“She is wearing this red dress.”*
(Adjust your prompt depending on the image you’re using.)

**Bonus Tips:**

- Crop out people’s heads if you’re trying to make your model wear clothes from another person’s photo.

- If your image isn’t cropped to **9:16** (the same ratio as your model image), the final result may appear zoomed in or stretched.

- Instead of uploading multiple clothing items into separate boxes inside the Omni Reference box, use **Canva** to combine them into **one single image** using a **9:16 template**.
