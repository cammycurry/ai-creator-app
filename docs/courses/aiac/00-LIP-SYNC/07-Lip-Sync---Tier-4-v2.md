## Content

**If you want to keep everything on the same platform, I would definitely recommend this lip sync method.** It’s reliable, affordable, and the quality is top-notch.

The only downside is that lip sync is limited to the clip duration (5 or 10 seconds). For longer, uncut lip sync, use the **Lip Sync Tier 3** method.
