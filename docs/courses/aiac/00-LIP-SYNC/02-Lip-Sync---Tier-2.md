You can upload up to 30 seconds of audio and video to Pixverse. Since OpenArt AI only gives us 10-second videos, you have two options:

- Upload the same 10-second video three times to match a 30-second audio clip.

- Upload one 10-second video with the full 30-second audio.

- Pixverse works with **1080p, 9:16 MP4 videos** and accepts an **MP3 file** for audio.

If the audio is longer than the video, the video will start playing in reverse after it ends. This is fine for static scenes (like when the model is sitting or standing still). But if there’s movement, like walking, driving, or anything in one direction, it will look unnatural when it starts reversing after 10 seconds.

When I have moving scenes, I prefer to upload more short clips (10 seconds each) to match the length of the audio. But if my avatar is just sitting and talking, I’m okay uploading one 10-second video with the full audio, even if it loops in reverse.
