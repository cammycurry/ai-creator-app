## Content

When doing lip sync, make sure to include background music (just adjust the volume so it doesn’t interfere with what's being said). Also, add natural background sounds that match the scene.

Inside CapCut, click on **Audio > Sound Effects**, and you can search for the type of background sound you want.

**Examples:**

- If Madi is walking next to a river: I’d include the sound effect of a river flowing.

- If Madi is walking in a public area: I’d add the sound of people talking in the background.

- If Madi is driving a car: I’d include the sound effect "inside a moving car."

Adding these sound effects will drastically improve the realism and emotional impact of your video.
