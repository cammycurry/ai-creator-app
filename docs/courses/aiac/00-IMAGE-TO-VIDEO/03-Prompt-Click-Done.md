## Content

Important:

- Since character training is no longer required, creating a 1-click story is simple. Just go to **Story → Character Vlog → Custom**, upload an image of your avatar, enter your prompt, and select the appropriate settings. (The avatar will appear wearing the same outfit shown in the uploaded image.)

- Before clicking **Create**, you can also select **Preview** to see what the final result could look like. From there, you can edit, delete, or add images before the software generates the full story.

- Once your 1-story click is complete, most clips will automatically be set to 1.5x speed. Unless you’re going for a slow-motion or fast-forward effect on purpose, adjust the speed (typically down to 1.0–1.2x) so the movement looks natural and lifelike.

- This method can use a lot of credits if you’re fine-tuning it. I recommend doing most of the work manually, but it’s worth testing occasionally, it can generate a full storytelling video in just minutes.
