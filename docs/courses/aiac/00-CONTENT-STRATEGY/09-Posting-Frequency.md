When it comes to Reels, if you are below a few thousand followers, Reels should be your main focus in order to get more reach. If you want to grow the fastest, one Reel a day is not enough, especially if your Reels are 5, 6, 7 seconds long. The more exposure you get, the more chances you have to reach a wider audience. And since we have AI, we have the advantage that we can create content fast or even in bulk. I feel like especially when you're starting out, you want to force the algorithm to see you, to notice you, to push your content onto people's For You pages. One video a day is simply not enough. When I started, I was doing two mostly, sometimes even three a day, and then I quickly started growing because this AI niche was so new. And then I built Ika and was busy with that all the time, so I pulled back to one video a day. My goal and my wish for my own content is always to post two to three times a day. I think this is optimal, unless you're doing some kind of storytelling videos or more complicated videos that are 20, 30 seconds or longer. Then maybe one piece of content a day is enough. But in general, if you're creating content less than 10 seconds long, two to three posts a day is what I would say is optimal.
