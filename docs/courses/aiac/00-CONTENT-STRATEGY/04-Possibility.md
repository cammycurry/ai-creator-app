When it comes to possibility-type content, this means that no matter whether you have results or not, this is something you can do from day one. You can show the power of AI what's possible and the benefits. For example, how quickly you can create something, how realistic you can make something, how much a photoshoot costs when you do it with AI compared to real life, how valuable it is to have an avatar so you don't have to film yourself, how much more convenient it is. I mean, if you've ever been a face-forward creator, you know that filming at home means trying to find the perfect corner where it's not messy, where everything looks aesthetic. You need to fix your hair, fix your clothes, fix the lighting. You record multiple times and still probably aren't happy with it. There's always background noise, kids playing, yelling, life happening. All of that is basically replaced with your avatar. You can literally create the same style of video and get the same message across while sitting on your sofa. These are all the different possibilities you can talk about, show, and highlight so people can see, okay, this is what I'm missing if I'm not on board. Showing possibilities is one of the things I've been doing from the very beginning in my content, even before I had any proof. I would show the realism of my avatar, the emotional tone in her voice, realistic movements, expressions. Whether it was reels or stories, I would always show and talk a lot about what's possible with this kind of business model. And here's another part. Since this is AI, you can show two types of possibilities. First, the content side. How convenient and powerful AI-created content is. Second, the business side. Why digital marketing? Why selling digital products? What are the benefits? For example, with digital products, you create something once and you can sell it an infinite number of times. You set up automation, pump out content, drive traffic to your links, and people can buy even when you're not online. If you've watched the Sleep Selling module, you already know. You can literally wake up every day with a few hundred dollars already made. That's not possible with a 9-to-5 or with many other types of businesses. Or if you're an affiliate for ICA, the benefits are huge. You already have a brand behind you. You don't need to worry about the product, the updates, or even taking care of the community. You just drive traffic to your link and make sales for a product you didn't even create, and you get 80% commission. It doesn't get much better than that. You have zero responsibility but all the benefits. Or maybe you're using your avatar for a different business, coaching, consulting, whatever. You can talk about the benefits of your business. So whatever it is, showing the possibilities when you don't yet have proof is what eventually gives you the proof. And even later, when you do have proof, showing possibilities is always effective for new people, both on the business side and the AI side.
