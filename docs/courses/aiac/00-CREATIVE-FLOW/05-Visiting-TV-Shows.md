This is the prompt I used for all of my videos:

''she runs to another scene while front facing the camera. camera handheld following the motion of her extended hand like she is filming herself in selfie mode. ''

Once I had all the videos ready, I stitched them inside capcut, turned on screenrecording and went on youtube to find all of the theme songs for each of these tv shows. Then added the sound inside capcut to match the clips and added fade in and fade out effect in between the songs. (0.5 sec fade in and 0.5 sec face out.)
