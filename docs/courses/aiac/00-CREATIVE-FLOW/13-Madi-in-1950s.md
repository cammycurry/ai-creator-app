Base prompt:

Ultra-detailed RAW-style portrait of an elegant woman with long wavy blonde hair and blue eyes, seated at a mahogany cocktail bar in a muted 1950s setting. The light is soft and neutral, slightly cool with visible grain and natural shadows. She wears a fitted black satin cocktail dress cut to just above the knee, long opera gloves, pearl necklace, diamond drop earrings, and a single diamond bracelet worn correctly over one glove. Subtle seams of sheer nylon stockings are visible, adding period-accurate detail. A wide-brim black hat casts a soft shadow across her eyes. One gloved hand holds a slim vintage cigarette holder with a faint curl of smoke; the other rests beside a folded 1950s newspaper whose bold serif headline reads “Modern Heiress Breaks $290K Record.” She sits poised with her legs crossed at the knee, posture elegant and composed. Behind her, a bartender in a crisp white jacket polishes crystal glasses while several well-dressed men in dark tailored suits and fedoras stand nearby, glancing in her direction with courteous admiration. The atmosphere is subdued—charcoal, beige, and deep brown tones—with wood grain, smoke haze, and real unfiltered texture capturing the quiet sophistication of mid-century nightlife.
