Base prompt:

Ultra-realistic close up filling the screen full-body 4K portrait of that woman standing beside a very large, human-size teddy bear, both fully visible and the same height. That teddy bear (from the reference image) stands upright next to her. She’s smiling brightly toward the camera, her expression warm and joyful, radiating feminine confidence and happiness. Make that green heart that teddy bear is holding like a pillow material

Above her head float light-pink glossy balloons arranged neatly to form the numbers “4000”, their delicate strings hanging down and softly reflecting the studio light. The balloons shimmer subtly against the clean white background, giving the scene a light, celebratory feel.

She’s wearing a fitted pastel-pink ribbed crop tank and matching high-waisted leggings, showing her fit, athletic, curvy build with slightly defined lines and natural femininity. On her feet, she wears white Nike sneakers, keeping the look casual and chic. Her long blonde hair is styled to one side in soft waves, falling gracefully over her shoulder.

She accessorizes with gold jewelry — small hoop earrings, a dainty necklace, and a thin bracelet that catches the light naturally. Her nails are glossy light pink, matching her outfit perfectly.

The white studio background is clean, evenly lit, with gentle shadows that define form without harsh contrast. The composition feels elegant yet playful — modern lifestyle luxury with a touch of soft joy. The scene captures a confident, happy woman celebrating success, standing proudly beside her oversized teddy companion, framed in pastel tones and glowing natural light.
