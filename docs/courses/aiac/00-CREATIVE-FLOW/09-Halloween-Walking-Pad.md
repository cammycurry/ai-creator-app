Base prompt:

Full-body image of that woman, captured at 1 meter, tightly framed from head to toe, walking on a sleek black walking pad inside a modern luxury living room at night. The camera meets her eye-level, and she’s looking directly into the lens with a calm, warm smile. Her blonde hair flows in soft waves, parted naturally, tucked gently behind one ear. She wears a cozy pastel pink off-shoulder crop top embroidered with tiny jack-o'-lantern faces and stars. Her matching knit shorts are printed with soft black spiderwebs and have ribbon-tied sides. Around her neck: a dainty choker with a dangling ghost charm. On her feet: pink-striped fuzzy socks and plush slippers with stitched mini bats. Her nails are glossy blush with candy, spider, and ghost decals, peeking out as she holds a soft pink pumpkin-shaped mug. Background styling: A glowing pink neon sign on the wall: “Cute & Spooky”. Stacked pumpkins near a ghost-printed blanket on the sofa. Mini spiderweb overlays draped along the top shelf. A framed sketch of a smiling ghost next to a soft lamp. Fairy lights line the floor molding with tiny bat-shaped bulbs.

Then I used that image and just changed the outfit by giving this image to chat gpt and said:

''I will give you an image that I am going with just for you to see it but I am going to need you just to change the outfits now with new prompts so you will write something like: 'just make her clothes wearing this and that...' you just fill up the entire prompt of her next cute halloween outfit. change her style, hairstyle, what she wears, what she holds, in some even add some halloween make up. make her always cute not scary, in some sexy, but not nfsw triggering.''

I didn't always get what I wanted so I keep giving more instructions to chatgpt about what I want and don't want.

Just make her clothes a fitted black satin corset top with structured boning and pastel pink velvet ribbon lacing up the front, paired with a micro skater skirt in dusty blush leather with stitched candy heart appliqués scattered across the hem. Her legs are bare but glowing, and her hair is styled in a sleek high ponytail wrapped in a soft black ribbon bow. Around her neck is a silver chain necklace with a dripping candy pendant. In her left hand, she holds a miniature structured handbag shaped like a ghost, with a pearl handle and silver charm details. Her nails are glossy jet black with pink candy decals and stitched stars. Then when I had these 10 images I run them all with Kling 2.1 Pro (5sec) by typing this 2 prompts for all of the images:

Prompt 1: she is walking at a normal pace on a stationary walking pad. She is happy. camera static.

Prompt 2: she is walking at a normal pace on a stationary walking pad. She is happy and playful. camera static.

Once I had 2 videos for each outfit (total of 20 videos) then I picked the best and edited in capcut.

I tried to change outfits on every beat drop by also adjusting the walk so it looks like it's more fluid.

To save some credits go with fewer outfits.
