Base Prompt:

Hyper-realistic 4K full-body image of a stunning blonde woman with long messy hair, glossy light pink nails, and layered silver chains. She wears ripped black jeans, a fitted black leather jacket, and a cropped white AC/DC t-shirt tied in the front with an open neckline. She stands with confident posture and a subtle, captivating smile in front of a massive graffiti wall with “$280K” spray-painted in raw urban style. In the background, two jacked, fit, handsome men with tattoos, dressed in black, graffiti the wall — one with a sharp short fade haircut, the other with a slightly longer fade. The scene has a gritty urban backdrop, moody daylight, cinematic lighting, hyper-detailed textures, and radiates fierce feminine rock star energy.
