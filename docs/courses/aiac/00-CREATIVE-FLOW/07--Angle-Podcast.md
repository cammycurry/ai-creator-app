close up of that woman on a modern luxury podcast. wearing high pony tail, modern off shoulder light pink long sleeve top wearing a necklase of a silver cross. long light pink nails resting on the table. microphone front of her. looking past the camera. camera diagonal. subtle smile, teeth barelly visible

I created few different angles and then upscaled them all in Lupa.

I used these 4:
