Some of the multiangle prompts (I didn't use all of them):

- Close-Up Detail Shot

“A macro close-up of that woman’s elegant hand with diamond jewelry brushing over the Porsche crest logo on the hood of that red GT3 RS, castle stones softly blurred in the background. Captured with ultra-sharp RAW textures highlighting the jewelry sparkle, car’s glossy paint, and her smooth skin.”

- Front Hood Sitting

“A mid-shot from a slightly low angle of that blonde woman in her fitted red dress sitting gracefully on the hood of that red Porsche GT3 RS, looking directly at the camera with the castle towering behind her. Captured in RAW with soft diffused daylight, sharp detail on her jewelry, the car’s curves, and the stone textures.”

- Side Angle Hood Pose

“A cinematic side-profile shot of that woman sitting on the hood of the red GT3 RS, one hand resting elegantly on the windshield edge, her long blonde hair flowing naturally. Captured in RAW with crisp focus on the car’s sleek lines, her dress texture, and the castle’s stone façade.”

- Wing Interaction

“A three-quarter rear shot of that blonde woman in her red dress touching the carbon-fiber rear wing of the Porsche GT3 RS, castle walls looming above. Captured in RAW with sharp detail on the wing’s finish, her jewelry sparkle, and the dramatic stone architecture.”

- Opening the Door

“A mid-shot of that woman reaching for the driver’s door handle of the red Porsche GT3 RS, her jewelry glinting in the soft daylight, castle stones forming the backdrop. Captured in RAW with sharp detail on the handle, reflections on the glossy door, and her confident stance.”

- Entering the Car

“A wide-angle shot of that woman stepping elegantly into the red Porsche GT3 RS, one hand on the roofline and the other adjusting her dress, the castle standing tall in the background. Captured in RAW with sharp focus on her jewelry, the car’s curves, and the refined luxury setting.”

- Inside the Car Portrait

“A close-up interior shot of that blonde woman sitting in the driver’s seat of the Porsche GT3 RS, hands on the wheel, looking directly at the camera through the open door. Captured in RAW with crisp focus on her jewelry, the steering wheel details, and soft light filtering across her face.”

- Car Detail Shot

“A macro shot of the Porsche GT3 RS wheel and brake caliper, with that woman’s red-dressed silhouette softly reflected in the glossy paint. Captured in RAW with sharp textures on the wheel, metallic details, and reflections.”

- Castle Driveway Wide Shot

“A wide establishing shot of that woman standing proudly beside the red Porsche GT3 RS in front of the grand castle driveway, her red dress echoing the car’s color. Captured in RAW with crisp detail on the stone castle, the car’s curves, and her elegant figure.”

- Over-the-Shoulder Car View

“An over-the-shoulder shot of that blonde woman in her fitted red dress, facing the red Porsche GT3 RS with her hand extended toward the hood emblem, castle architecture filling the background. Captured in RAW with sharp focus on her jewelry, hair texture, and the glossy emblem.”

- Necklace Close-Up

“A macro close-up editorial shot of that woman’s diamond necklace with a ruby pendant resting elegantly against her fitted red dress, captured in RAW with sharp focus on the jewel’s sparkle and fabric texture, the castle stone softly blurred in the background.”

- Walking Side Angle at Hip Level

“A dynamic close-up side shot framed at hip level of that woman walking gracefully in her fitted red dress, her arm and jewelry in crisp detail, with the red Porsche GT3 RS partially blurred behind her for depth, captured in RAW with cinematic lens realism.”

- Face Close-Up from the Side

“A close-up side portrait of that woman with long blonde waves and clear eyes, her face softly turned in profile, captured in RAW with sharp focus on her natural expression, hair texture, and jewelry, with the castle wall forming a muted editorial backdrop.”
