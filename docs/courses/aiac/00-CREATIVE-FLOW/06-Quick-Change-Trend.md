Prompt: mirror selfie of that woman holding a light pink iphone 16 pro max covering her face. hair in high long pony tail hair coming over the shoulder. she has long light pink nails. she is wearing a nike sports bra light pink. she is visible from hips up. she is in her modern luxury living room. wearing earings, rings, and necklase from van cleef and bracelet. medium to large chest

![openart-image_e70COy2X_1764795622920_raw.png](https://assets.skool.com/f/50266cea381e4660ae9b2945179d86e1/15335a7dd29d47aeaae4a7576f58aa920c0af22c65df496ca8755f7cb07c4012-md.png)Then I found 5 different outfits on pinterest:

![916 (66).png](https://assets.skool.com/f/50266cea381e4660ae9b2945179d86e1/6e80bcaebbdb417ca966b4230ea3022c28fb7eb616fc4354bfa5aafc013f2162-md.png)Then I used my new refference image (the one in pink nike sports bra) and add one of these outfit images in the next refference box and typed this:

''replace her clothes''

I run the same prompt 5 times for 5 different outfits and got this:

![916 (65).png](https://assets.skool.com/f/50266cea381e4660ae9b2945179d86e1/4bb093d58f384bb4837ecfb8018847e54e2ca061fac843b2945f901448fd6f47-md.png)Then I used Start + End frame method with Kling 2.1 PRO (5sec) to create transitions:

- I uploaded the first image and the second image and typed this with kling:

''person stands still while her clothes transform.''

- Then I put the second image at the start frame, and uploaded third images as the end frame and typed the same prompt:

''person stands still while her clothes transform.''

- I repeated that for all and at the end I use the first image as the last frame too so the video looks like it's in a loop.

Then I just uploaded all to capcut in order and bump the speed to 3x.

I saved the video, uploaded again to capcut, and add a zoom in and zoom out throughout the entire clip.
