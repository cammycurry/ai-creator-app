**Prompt:**

Cinematic handheld shot captured with a 35mm lens, shallow depth of field for a natural, immersive perspective. The camera begins low, tracking her steady steps on the walking pad, then gently dollies upward with a smooth, floating motion, stabilizing near chest level. Subtle natural camera shake adds realism as she continues her graceful pace. She maintains relaxed posture, her movements fluent and confident, occasionally shifting her gaze slightly to the side and then forward with a warm, friendly smile. Her hair moves naturally with each step. Soft daylight fills the modern interior, with gentle focus transitions that emphasize natural motion and depth. The overall tone feels calm, confident, and warmly engaging — an authentic, candid moment full of quiet energy and ease.

**Negative prompt:**

blurry, low fps, low frame rate, unnatural motion, flicker, glitch, bad hands, missing fingers, makeup, beauty filter, slow motion, distorted face, robotic motion, excessive direct eye contact, frozen expression, overexposed lighting, inconsistent body proportions, warped perspective, jerky transitions, oversaturated colors
