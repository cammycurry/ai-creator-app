![openart-image_y54AgvE-_1768655955913_raw.jpg](https://assets.skool.com/f/50266cea381e4660ae9b2945179d86e1/57180a1c910c4cb3b740fbc9573d05b1d6501e48575e4c3a977e7c1b58c58445-md.jpg)I did similar thing with the image of her putting on slippers, and her watching tv and standing next to the window.

Once I had 10+ different images I just run them all through kling 2.6 by typing very simple prompts like 'she is pouring the tee', 'she is watching tv', 'she is putting on slippers', etc...With every single prompt I like to have camera to have some motion so I typed prompts like 'camera handheld with visible motion', or 'camera handheld with visible motion pans around'.

Once I had all videos ready I just stitched them all together inside Capcut/edits. Cropped them under 1 second per clips and added a suitable background song.
