## Saved Alternatives

### Option A — "The all-in-one AI influencer studio"
- Tool-first positioning, premium feel
- Sub: "Create custom AI creators. Generate photos, videos, and talking-head content — all with the same face, every time. One tool replaces your entire stack."
- Pros: "AI influencer" is #1 search term, "all-in-one" addresses fragmentation, "studio" sounds premium
- Cons: "Studio" might be generic, doesn't mention money opportunity

### Option C — "One app. Custom AI creators. Endless content."
- Punchy three-beat rhythm, confident
- Sub: "Design your own AI influencer. Generate photorealistic photos, videos, and talking-head clips with the same face every time. Manage 1 or 100 creators from one dashboard."
- Badge: "AI influencer market is worth $6.95B and growing"
- Pros: Rhythm is catchy, "1 or 100" speaks to agencies, badge creates urgency
- Cons: "Custom AI creators" might confuse newcomers

### Money-First (from earlier brainstorm — for blog/SEO, not hero)
- "AI influencers are making $10K–$100K/month"
- "Create AI influencers that make money"
- Better for SEO articles and blog posts, not the main hero

---

## Key Decisions
- Lead with what the tool DOES, not the money opportunity (save that for below fold)
- "AI influencer" must be in the H1 (it's the #1 search term)
- "Same face every time" is the key differentiator — always mention it
- "Manage one or a hundred" speaks to agencies without alienating newcomers
- Don't name competitor tools in the hero (save for a comparison section further down)
- Showcase grid uses real generated images from our roster (Sienna, Valentina, Jordyn, Nara, Camila, Marcus)
