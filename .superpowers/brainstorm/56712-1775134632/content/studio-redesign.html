What changed

1. "Your World" replaces "MY REFS"

Label explains what refs ARE: "Click to use in generation. Keeps your content consistent." 3-column compact grid with checkmarks on active refs. No separate tabs for now — just your stuff.

2. Inspiration upload is clear

Big drop zone with clear label: "Drop a photo here — make her do THIS." Not hidden at the bottom.

3. Recent history IN the panel

Last 3-5 generations with thumbnail, type badge, and prompt preview. Click to reload those settings. No separate "history page" — it's right there while you're creating.

4. Pills not tabs for content type

Rounded pills feel less rigid than full-width tabs. Same function, friendlier feel. No cost text cluttering the pill — shows in footer when selected.

5. Input box with integrated ref tags

One card-style input: prompt on top, attached refs shown as chips below. "refs auto-applied from left" hint connects the two panels. No mystery about how refs work.

6. Config is inline, not a "SETTINGS" block

Photo count is a compact row, not a labeled section. Feels like part of the flow, not a config panel.

7. Consistent coloring

Matches the dashboard: white surface, #FAFAFA background, #EBEBEB borders, terracotta accent. No more mismatched styling.
