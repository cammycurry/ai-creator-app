Key improvements over the first mockup

1. Unlimited references — no caps on how many. Add backgrounds, products, outfits, poses, custom. Scroll and search.
2. Smart auto-matching — template says "gym"? System auto-attaches your gym ref. You just confirm.
3. Usage tracking — "Used in 12 posts" tells you which refs are building consistency. See your world grow.
4. Video-ready — same references work for video later. Background ref → Kling instead of Gemini. Same concept.
5. Accessible everywhere — from sidebar, from creator profile, AND inside the studio. Not hidden in one place.
6. Mobile-first — grid collapses to 2 columns. Studio slide list stacks vertically. Touch-friendly 44px targets.
